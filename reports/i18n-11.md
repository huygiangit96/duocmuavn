# I18N-11 - Dich Checkout va Xac Nhan Don Hang

## Muc tieu

Dich trang checkout va trang xac nhan don hang bang `next-intl`, dong thoi dung navigation helper de giu locale.

## File da cap nhat

- Dung `Link` va `useRouter` tu `@/i18n/navigation`.
