# I18N-5 - Seed du lieu tieng Anh mau

## Muc tieu

Tao management command de dien du lieu tieng Anh mau vao cac field `_en` cua ban ghi hien co.

## File da tao

- `backend/core/management/commands/seed_en.py`

## Noi dung seed

### Category

Cap nhat `name_en` cho 6 danh muc hien co.

Command ho tro ca mapping theo ten trong prompt va mapping theo slug prototype hien co:

- `may-thiet-bi` -> `Agricultural Equipment`
- `kich-thich-sinh-truong` -> `Plant Growth Regulators`
- `thuoc-tru-benh` -> `Fungicides`
- `thuoc-tru-nhuyen-the` -> `Molluscicides`
- `thuoc-tru-sau` -> `Insecticides`
- `thuoc-tru-tuyen-trung` -> `Nematicides`

### Plant

Cap nhat `name_en` cho 7 cay trong hien co:

- Rice
- Coffee
- Pepper
- Fruit Trees
- Vegetables
- Field Crops
- Cashew

Command cung co mapping bo sung cho `Cao su` va `Mia` neu sau nay co ban ghi nay.

### Product

Cap nhat 16 san pham hien co:

- `name_en`
- `short_desc_en`

Moi san pham co ten tieng Anh va mo ta ngan 1 cau.

### Article

Cap nhat 6 bai viet hien co:

- `title_en`
- `summary_en`

### SiteConfig

Cap nhat:

- `tagline_en = "Smart Solutions – Unique Products – Professional Service"`
- `about_en = "Duoc Mua GAH is a leading agricultural solutions provider in Vietnam."`

## Ket qua chay command

Da chay:

```powershell
$env:PYTHONIOENCODING='utf-8'; venv\Scripts\python.exe backend/manage.py seed_en
```

Ket qua:

```text
English seed completed: categories=6, plants=7, products=16, articles=6, site_configs=1 updated.
```

## Kiem tra

Da chay:

```powershell
venv\Scripts\python.exe backend\manage.py check
```

Ket qua:

```text
System check identified no issues (0 silenced).
```

## Ghi chu

- Command dung `update()`/`filter().update()` de cap nhat ban ghi hien co.
- Khong tao migration moi.
- Khong cap nhat `PROGRESS.md` vi day la prompt i18n bo sung.
