# I18N-12 - Dich Trang Tin Tuc

## Muc tieu

Dich trang danh sach tin tuc va trang chi tiet bai viet bang `next-intl`, dong thoi giu locale khi navigate.

## File da cap nhat

### `frontend/src/app/[locale]/tin-tuc/page.tsx`

- Import `useTranslations` tu `next-intl`.
- Dung `Link` va `useRouter` tu `@/i18n/navigation`.
- Thay text hardcode bang keys:
  - `news.title`
  - `news.search_placeholder`
  - `news.categories`
  - `product.all`
  - `common.loading`
  - `common.no_data`
  - `common.view_all`

### `frontend/src/app/[locale]/tin-tuc/[slug]/page.tsx`

- Import `useTranslations` tu `next-intl`.
- Dung `Link` va `useRouter` tu `@/i18n/navigation`.
- Thay text hardcode bang keys:
  - `news.views`
  - `news.published_at`
  - `news.latest`
  - `news.share`
  - `common.loading`
  - `common.error`
  - `common.no_data`
  - `common.view_all`
  - `nav.home`
  - `news.title`

### `frontend/messages/vi.json`

Them keys:

- `news.search_placeholder`
- `news.published_at`
- `news.share`

### `frontend/messages/en.json`

Them ban dich tieng Anh cho cac keys tren.
