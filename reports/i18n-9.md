# I18N-9 - Dich Trang Chu

## Muc tieu

Cap nhat trang chu de dung `next-intl` messages va giu locale khi navigate.

## File da cap nhat

### `frontend/src/app/[locale]/page.tsx`

- Import `useTranslations` tu `next-intl`.
- Doi `Link` tu `next/link` sang `@/i18n/navigation`.
- Them `const t = useTranslations()`.
- Dung translation keys cho cac phan chinh:
  - `home.banner_title`
  - `home.banner_sub`
  - `home.cta_contact`
  - `home.featured`
  - `home.categories`
  - `home.latest_news`
  - `common.view_all`
  - `cart.shop_now`
  - `header.hotline`
- Fallback hero dung `home.banner_title` va `home.banner_sub`.
- CTA hero va CTA cuoi trang dung messages.

### `frontend/messages/vi.json`

Them namespace `home`:

- `banner_title`
- `banner_sub`
- `cta_contact`
- `featured`
- `categories`
- `latest_news`

### `frontend/messages/en.json`

Them ban dich tieng Anh cho namespace `home`.
