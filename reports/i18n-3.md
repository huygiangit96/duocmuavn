# I18N serializers - query param lang

## Muc tieu

Cap nhat backend serializers de ho tro song ngu theo query param:

- `?lang=vi` hoac khong truyen param: tra ve field tieng Viet goc.
- `?lang=en`: tra ve field `_en` neu co noi dung, fallback ve field tieng Viet neu `_en` rong.

## Core

- Tao `backend/core/i18n_mixin.py`.
- Them `LangMixin.get_lang()`.
- Them `LangMixin.localized(obj, field_vi, field_en=None)`.

## Products serializers

Cap nhat `backend/apps/products/serializers.py`:

- `CategorySerializer.name`: localize `name` / `name_en`.
- `PlantSerializer.name`: localize `name` / `name_en`.
- `ProductListSerializer.name`: localize `name` / `name_en`.
- `ProductListSerializer.short_desc`: localize theo `short_desc_en`, fallback ve `description` vi model hien chua co cot `short_desc` tieng Viet.
- `ProductDetailSerializer.name`: localize `name` / `name_en`.
- `ProductDetailSerializer.description`: localize `description` / `description_en`.
- `ProductDetailSerializer.specs`: localize `specs` / `specs_en`.

## Schema support

Cap nhat `backend/apps/payments/views.py`:

- Them schema metadata cho VNPay/MoMo APIView de `spectacular --validate` khong con loi unable to guess serializer.
- Them response serializer `PaymentURLSerializer` va request serializer `MoMoIPNSerializer`.

## Kiem tra

Da chay:

```powershell
venv\Scripts\python.exe backend\manage.py check
$env:PYTHONIOENCODING='utf-8'; venv\Scripts\python.exe backend\manage.py spectacular --validate
```

- Django system check: PASS.
- Spectacular validation: PASS, Errors 0.
- Con 1 warning enum `status` collision tu drf-spectacular, khong phai loi schema.
